Places

  • Lingthem
    A very peaceful small village with a sparkling weather guarded by a small river
  • Aritar
    Aritar is blessed with lush green forests, sweeping hills of paddy fields and placid lakes hidden inside deep forests
  • Sillery Gaon
    Sillery Gaon is a picture-perfect Himalayan hamlet, with a gorgeous view of the mighty Mt. Kanchenjunga
  • Icche Gaon
    A new destination for nature lovers who want to spend few days in the lap of Himalayas
  • Rishi Khola
    Ever dreamt of a holiday filled with laziness, nature all around with deep lush green forests with chirps of never heard birds?
  • Phadamchen
    A tiny mountain village to be acclimatized before taking uphill towards Nathang Valley
  • Zuluk
    A small village beside a winding road, once a transit point to the historic Silk Route
  • Thambi View Point
    At a height of 11,200 ft, it offers a panoramic view of Mt. Kanchenjunga
  • Zig-Zag Road
    The famous zig-zag road (locally called Bhulbhulaiya for its 30 hairpin turns), can be viewed from Thambi View Point
  • Nathang Valley
    This lush valley was home to the traditional yak herders who came from Tibet. Few streams criss-cross the valley
  • Old Baba Mandir
    Also known as Baba Harbhajan Singh Temple,this is a must visit destination while touring the Old Silk Route
  • Kupup Lake
    There is something about high altitude lakes - you climb up the mountains, struggling through the hairpin bends and at the end, you are greeted by a gleam of water body
